Internet of Things

What is IoT, “The Internet of things”, is the intelligent connectivity of physical devices driving massive gains in efficiency, business growth, and quality of life.”

Why go for IoT?

IoT-based devices had a market of nearly 2 billion USD back in 2014 and it was more than 8 billion USD in 2020 but it is relatively a new field and one question that comes to mind is will IoT sustain in coming decades and is it worth investing time learning about it.

This question can be best answered by prof. Michael Nelson from Georgetown University, “It’s like determining the market size of plastics in the 1940s. At that time it was difficult to imagine that plastic could be everything.”

There is no one industry vertical where IoT will be used, it will be used everywhere from IT, consumer to Industry. We can say it will be the “Internet of Everything.”

Technically IoTs are smart systems and IoT are driven by a combination of

Sensors

Connectivity

People and Processes

These are connected with sensors and these sensors are pushing data to the cloud platform.
