Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Online Skill-Testing and Talent Search – An Intelligent Move to Assess and Appoint

Worldwide recruiters were aggressively looking for smart ways to scale-up their skill-testing and talent search initiatives until they realized that AI integration with their recruitment strategies can do wonders!

Technological advancements are disrupting global markets at a considerably fast-pace. These quick alterations have, however, helped Human Resource (HR) managers and Recruitment teams to streamline their hiring processes. Moreover, technology disruptions ensure an ease of anytime-anywhere access to human resources thus, improvising the overall recruitment tactics For instance, with evolvement of the Internet in 1995, various directory-based employee search platforms – like Monster.com and others – got mushroomed enabling recruiters to save their time on hiring quality workforce. Later after a decade, LinkedIn drastically transformed the hiring mechanism by establishing a unique professional social network to facilitate recruitment through network connections, recommendations, and endorsements.

AI's amalgamation with the recruitment space has aesthetically modified the overall hiring strategies of global enterprises. AI has been well recognized and adapted by numerous organization that leverage technology to advance their hiring metrics, thereby enabling flexibility of assessing candidates and appointing the best workforce.

Let's Get Familiar… with AI! AI facilitates machines, essentially computer systems, to learn from incidents or acquired information, apply rules to reach conclusions, adjust to the environment, and thus think or perform like humans. Through meaningful learning algorithms as well as neural networks, machines can be trained to execute particular tasks by processing huge data points and identifying relevant patterns.

Online Recruitment and AI

With the emergence of AI, recruiters are growingly embracing auto-proctored skill-testing assessments since they are pocket-friendly solutions, while simultaneously maintain integrity.

Advanced AI Features

Fast growing adoption of machine-learning, by global recruitment managers, is primarily based on the following smart features:

Auto-Proctoring

Auto-Proctoring is a boon to global enterprises for its time-saving, cost-effective, and credible solutions. Auto-Proctored skill-testing assessments reduce the hiring-budgets by nearly 40%, making this an affordable yet effective employee-testing option. Auto-Proctor performs like a human invigilator for candidates participating in skill-testing assessments from across the world. The technology has gained recognition for its interactive features that ensure a cheat-proof employee assessment and appointment environment.

Face Recognition

Brain-stormed by computer engineers and neuroscientists, the next-gen facial recognition feature integrated with AI, ensures a candidate's authenticity, while also affirms that no unethical means used during the assessment.

Worldwide recruiters are opting automated proctoring mechanism with smart facial recognition feature, as it:

Precisely detects participant's face in direct proportion to the large volume of images being fed to the system;

Timely raises red-flags to instigate any cheating instances;

idates' identity through the submitted valid government Photo-IDs and thus, further lowers any likelihood of imitation.

Voice Recognition

A voice-enabled Auto-Proctor intelligently recognizes audio for candidate's authentication and matches it to any background noise through an advanced graphical representation of the speech patterns, thus to further eliminate any possible cheating instances.

Accelerating Digital Transformation through AI

AI has up-scaled the integrity and authenticity of online skill-testing evaluations, thus ensuring a one-stop solution to HR teams for their online employee assessment and workforce appointment initiatives.

To Sum-up

Numerous technological advancements have empowered AI taking massive-leap by eliminating human invigilators for assessing and appointing appropriate workforce. Also, worldwide hiring managers have recognized AI as an efficient, affordable, and scalable solution and thus, strategically adopting it as a critical-part of their recruitment tactics.

Moreover, AI has gained momentum, while manually proctored skill-testing assessment is at a verge of phase-out in the coming four to five years, thus paving strong pedestal for an intelligently automated employee-testing and appointment model.
