Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Online Skill-Testing and Talent Search – An Intelligent Move to Assess and Appoint

Worldwide recruiters were aggressively looking for smart ways to scale-up their skill-testing and talent search initiatives until they realized that AI integration with their recruitment strategies can do wonders!

Technological advancements are disrupting global markets at a considerably fast-pace. These quick alterations have, however, helped Human Resource (HR) managers and Recruitment teams to streamline their hiring processes. Moreover, technology disruptions ensure an ease of anytime-anywhere access to human resources thus, improvising the overall recruitment tactics For instance, with evolvement of the Internet in 1995, various directory-based employee search platforms – like Monster.com and others – got mushroomed enabling recruiters to save their time on hiring quality workforce. Later after a decade, LinkedIn drastically transformed the hiring mechanism by establishing a unique professional social network to facilitate recruitment through network connections, recommendations, and endorsements.

AI's amalgamation with the recruitment space has aesthetically modified the overall hiring strategies of global enterprises. AI has been well recognized and adapted by numerous organization that leverage technology to advance their hiring metrics, thereby enabling flexibility of assessing candidates and appointing the best workforce.

Let's Get Familiar… with AI! AI facilitates machines, essentially computer systems, to learn from incidents or acquired information, apply rules to reach conclusions, adjust to the environment, and thus think or perform like humans. Through meaningful learning algorithms as well as neural networks, machines can be trained to execute particular tasks by processing huge data points and identifying relevant patterns.
